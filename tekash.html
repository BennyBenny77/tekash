The Tekash Blueprint

Introduction

Business Name: Tekash — Tanzania Cultural Experience  |  Location: Dar es Salaam, Tanzania  |  Phone: +255 696 575 025

Tekash is a cultural tourism business that focuses on providing tourists with authentic Tanzanian cultural activities, including traditional dances, local food experiences, village tours, and cultural education programmes.

We believe Tanzania's greatest tourism asset is its people and their living cultural traditions. From the Maasai of the north to the vibrant coastal Swahili culture, Tekash curates genuine, respectful, and affordable cultural encounters that leave lasting memories for every visitor.

Business Objectives

General Objective: To promote Tanzanian culture while generating profit through tourism activities that benefit the local community.

Specific Objectives:

  • To attract local and international tourists to Tanzanian cultural experiences
  • To create employment opportunities for youth in the tourism sector
  • To preserve and promote Tanzanian traditions and culture for future generations

Market Analysis

Tourism in Tanzania continues to grow because of national parks, mountains like Kilimanjaro, beaches of Zanzibar, and rich cultural attractions. Many tourists are interested in learning about authentic African traditions and lifestyles. Therefore, cultural tourism has a strong and growing market opportunity in Tanzania.

Target Customers:

  • International Tourists — Primary market with highest spending power and strong interest in authentic cultural experiences
  • Local Tanzanian Tourists — Growing domestic market increasingly interested in cultural heritage tourism
  • Students — School and university groups seeking cultural and educational field experiences
  • Researchers — Academics, documentary makers, and anthropologists seeking authentic access to Tanzanian culture

Marketing Strategy

Tekash will use the following marketing channels to reach both local and international audiences and promote our cultural tourism services effectively:

  • Social Media Marketing — Active Facebook and Instagram pages with regular cultural content, tour highlights, and customer testimonials
  • Website Advertisements — Professional company website with online booking, Google visibility, and SEO content marketing
  • Posters & Brochures — Physical marketing materials distributed at hotels, airports, and tourist information centres in Dar es Salaam
  • Partnerships with Hotels and Tour Companies — Referral and co-marketing agreements to cross-promote Tekash tours to hotel guests

Operational Plan

Tekash will organise the following core cultural experiences, each carefully planned with community partners, trained guides, and quality cultural materials to ensure authenticity and high visitor satisfaction:

  • Village Cultural Tours — Guided visits to authentic Tanzanian villages with interactive community engagement and storytelling
  • Traditional Dance Events — Live performances by certified cultural troupes showcasing regional Tanzanian dance traditions
  • Local Food Experiences — Hands-on cooking sessions and food tastings featuring traditional Tanzanian recipes and local ingredients
  • Handcraft Exhibitions — Curated marketplace showcasing and selling authentic Tanzanian handcraft, art, and cultural souvenirs

Expected Benefits

By establishing Tekash as a leading cultural tourism operator in Dar es Salaam, the following benefits are expected for the business, its employees, and the broader Tanzanian community:

  • Profit Generation — Sustainable business revenue from tour packages, cultural event charges, and product sales
  • Employment Creation — Direct and indirect employment for guides, performers, drivers, craft sellers, and food suppliers
  • Promotion of Tanzanian Culture — Global visibility and appreciation of Tanzania's diverse cultural traditions and heritage
  • Increase in Tourism Activities — Contribution to the growth of Tanzania's tourism ecosystem and national economic development
